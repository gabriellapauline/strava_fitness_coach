### Progression rules
- **Load:** add 2.5 kg to barbell lifts when you hit the top rep count with clean form, for 2 consecutive sessions.
- **Goblet → Front Squat:** transition once 30 kg goblet for 10 reps feels controlled at the bottom.
- **Deload:** on run-plan deload weeks (Wk 4, Wk 12, Wk 16, Wk 20), drop gym loads 15–20% and skip the last set of each lift. Keep Hip Thrust and Face Pull in — they tolerate light work well and maintain the pattern.
